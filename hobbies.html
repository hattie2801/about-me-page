Hobbies

Here are a fun facts that I have learned over the years about each of my hobbies!

  • Cooking & Trying New Recipes - Honey is the only food that never spoils; it has been found in ancient Egyptian tombs still perfectly edible.
  • Reading - The longest novel ever written is À la recherche du temps perdu by Marcel Proust, with about 1.2 million words!
  • Writing Short Stories - Agatha Christie wrote some of her books while taking baths and eating apples for inspiration.
  • Music - Listening to music can enhance physical performance—runners who listen to music can run up to 15% longer.
  • Learning About Other Cultures - People who experience diverse cultural perspectives tend to develop more flexible thinking and problem-solving skills as well as increase creativity by rewiring the brain making it more adaptable an open-minded.
